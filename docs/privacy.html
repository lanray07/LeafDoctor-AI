LeafDoctor AI

Privacy Policy

LeafDoctor AI helps users track plant care, scan plant photos, and manage plant health routines. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information Stored On Your Device

LeafDoctor AI stores plant profiles, plant photos, scan history, care reminders, achievements, app settings, and subscription state locally on your device using Apple platform storage. This information is used to provide the app experience, including plant collections, care schedules, timelines, insights, and PDF care reports.

Photos and Camera Access

The app asks for camera or photo library access only when you choose to take, upload, scan, or save plant photos. Photos selected or captured in the app are used for plant health workflows and local care history.

AI Analysis

Mock AI mode is enabled by default. If a remote AI service is enabled in a future production backend, plant type, selected plant area, symptoms, notes, and encoded plant images may be sent to a secure backend endpoint for analysis. API keys should never be stored inside the app.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, LeafDoctor AI uses local notifications to remind you about watering, fertilising, pruning, repotting, misting, and pest checks. Notification settings can be changed in iOS Settings or inside the app where available.

Subscriptions and Purchases

LeafDoctor AI uses Apple's StoreKit services for subscription products and purchase status. Payment processing, subscription management, refunds, and billing records are handled by Apple according to Apple's own terms and privacy practices.

Data Sharing

LeafDoctor AI does not sell your personal information. The app is designed to work offline with local data. Data may be shared only when required to provide features you choose, such as Apple purchase validation, optional remote AI analysis if enabled, exporting a PDF report, or using the system share sheet.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete local app data from the app's settings. You can also remove the app from your device to delete locally stored app data. Subscription cancellation is managed through your Apple account.

Important Disclaimer

AI results are informational only and are not a guaranteed botanical diagnosis. Treatment outcomes may vary. Severe infestations, crop losses, toxic exposure concerns, or valuable greenhouse issues should be reviewed by qualified professionals.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, visit the LeafDoctor AI repository at https://github.com/lanray07/LeafDoctor-AI.
